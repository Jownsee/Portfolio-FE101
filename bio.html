About The Artist





Artist Bio

IONE is a Quezon City-based artist who graduated from the University of the Philippines Diliman College of Fine Arts, with a degree in Painting. His work is deeply introspective, often exploring the fluid and amorphous nature of identity. Using soot as his primary medium, IONE employs the fumage technique to create abstracted forms of smoke, embodying the transient and ever-changing aspects of the self. His art, with its dark undertones, serves as a powerful vehicle for documenting his inner dialogues and grappling with the complexities of existence.
