Book Cover Design

I teamed up with writers to create book covers that visually capture their stories. Using the fumage technique and the soot formations characteristic of my work, I crafted striking imagery, allowing the amorphous and abstract forms to convey the essence of each book's narrative. The result is a series of covers that not only draw readers in but also reflect the depth and nuances of the stories. Here are some of the covers I had the pleasure of designing.




Apparel Designs

Inspired by the fluid and ever-changing nature of smoke, I designed a series of dynamic and visually captivating apparels. Observing how smoke moves and transforms in the air, I translated these movements into unique and intriguing designs. Each T-shirt features a one-of-a-kind design, ensuring that every piece is as distinct as the smoke patterns that inspired it. The result is a collection of garments that are not only stylish but also carry a sense of motion and depth. Below are some examples of the designs I developed for this project.
