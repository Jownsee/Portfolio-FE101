Mushroomscapes Series

In this series, IONE explores the potential of soot printing to generate multiple variations of a single image. Utilizing a single stencil, several prints have been produced, each uniquely distinct. The variability of the fumage process—driven by the fluid nature of air and soot—ensures that no two prints are identical. This interplay between technique and chance results in a collection of artworks that, while stemming from the same stencil, each possess their own distinct character and nuance.
